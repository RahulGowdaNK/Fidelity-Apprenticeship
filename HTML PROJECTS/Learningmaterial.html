Introduction to Rasagulla

Rasagulla is a traditional Indian dessert made from ball shaped dumplings of chenna(an indian cottage cheese) and semolina dough, cooked in light syrup made of sugar. It's a popular sweet,especially in the eastern states of india like West Bengal and odisha.

Key Ingridients:-
* Chenna
* Sugar syrup
* Traditionally flavored with cardmom and rose water.
* Popular during festivals and celebration.

The making of Rasagulla is an ancient has been perfected over generation.
